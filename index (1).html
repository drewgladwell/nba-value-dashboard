What is BPM? Box Plus/Minus estimates a player's contribution per 100 possessions compared to a league-average player. A score of +5.0 means the team performs 5 points better per 100 possessions with that player on the floor. League average = 0.0. Stars typically range from +4 to +10.

Value Score = (BPM ÷ Annual Salary in $M) × 10. Higher = more impact per dollar spent. Players on rookie contracts often top this list because their production outpaces their pay.
